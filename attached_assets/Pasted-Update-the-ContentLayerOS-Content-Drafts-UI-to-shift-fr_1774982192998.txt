Update the ContentLayerOS "Content Drafts" UI to shift from content-type grouping to a structured publishing sequence.

---

## GOAL

Transform the UI from:

→ “List of content drafts”

Into:

→ “Execution-ready content plan”

Each item should clearly tell the user:

* What to post
* Where to post
* When to post
* What to do immediately after posting

---

## NEW UI STRUCTURE

Group content into 3 sections:

---

# SECTION 1: 🚀 DAY 1 LAUNCH

This is the highest-priority section and should always appear at the top.

---

Label:
"LinkedIn Article (Authority / Pulse)"

"Publish FIRST"

* Title
* Full article content

Add helper text:

"Post this as a LinkedIn Article. This builds authority and long-term visibility (SEO + AI citations)."

Label:
"#1 LinkedIn Feed Post"

"Post 5–10 minutes AFTER article"

* Post content

* Reduce thinking required by user
* Make sequence obvious
* Make actions clear
* Make this feel like a playbook, not a CMS

* Copy buttons

Only change presentation + add metadata

# END STATE

1. Open page
2. Follow steps top to bottom
3. Execute full content strategy without thinking
